Breathwork Guide

Step-by-step breathing techniques to calm your nervous system

Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)

Perfect for anxiety and overthinking

  1. Inhale through nose for 4 counts
  2. Hold for 4 counts
  3. Exhale through mouth for 4 counts
  4. Hold empty for 4 counts
  5. Repeat for 5-10 rounds

4-7-8 Breathing

Deeply calming for rest and sleep

  1. Exhale completely through mouth
  2. Inhale through nose for 4 counts
  3. Hold breath for 7 counts
  4. Exhale through mouth for 8 counts
  5. Repeat for 4 cycles

Heart-Centered Breathing

Opens your heart to feminine energy

  1. Place both hands over your heart
  2. Close your eyes, breathe naturally
  3. Imagine breathing through your heart
  4. Inhale love and softness
  5. Exhale tension and resistance
  6. Continue for 5+ minutes

Nutrition Guide

Sample meals and supplement guides for feminine wellness

Sample Day: High-Fat, Low-Carb

Breakfast: Eggs cooked in butter with avocado and smoked salmon

Lunch: Large salad with grilled chicken, olive oil, nuts, and full-fat cheese

Dinner: Grass-fed steak with roasted vegetables in ghee

Snacks: Macadamia nuts, olives, hard-boiled eggs, full-fat yogurt

Essential Supplements

  • Vitamin D3 with K2: 2,000-5,000 IU daily
  • Magnesium Bis-glycinate: 300-400mg before bed
  • Omega-3 (Fish Oil): High-quality, 2-3g daily
  • Apple Cider Vinegar: 1 tbsp in water before meals
  • Electrolytes: Sodium, potassium, magnesium daily

Quick Meal Ideas

  • Zucchini noodles with pesto and grilled chicken
  • Cauliflower rice stir-fry with coconut oil
  • Salmon with asparagus in butter sauce
  • Lettuce-wrapped burgers with avocado
  • Bone broth with vegetables and herbs
  • Greek yogurt bowl with nuts and berries
Flow

Movement Guide

Simple routines for strength, flexibility, and grace

Morning Movement Routine (10 min)

  1. Sun Salutations: 3-5 rounds (5 min)
  2. Cat-Cow Stretches: 10 rounds (2 min)
  3. Hip Circles: 10 each direction (1 min)
  4. Spinal Twists: 5 each side (2 min)

Practice daily to awaken your body with grace

Strength Training Basics

3-4 times per week, 20-30 minutes

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Planks: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 each leg
  • Glute Bridges: 3 sets of 15 reps

Evening Wind-Down

  1. Gentle Yoga: 10 minutes of restorative poses
  2. Child's Pose: 3 minutes, breathe deeply
  3. Legs Up the Wall: 5 minutes
  4. Savasana: 5 minutes, total relaxation

Release the day and prepare for rest

Ground

Grounding Guide

Practical techniques to reconnect with the earth

Daily Grounding Practice

10-20 minutes, ideally in the morning

  1. Remove shoes and socks
  2. Stand or walk barefoot on grass, sand, or soil
  3. Feel the texture beneath your feet
  4. Take slow, deep breaths
  5. Notice sensations: temperature, moisture, texture
  6. Allow tension to drain into the earth

5-Minute Grounding Emergency

When you're anxious or overwhelmed

  • 5-4-3-2-1 Technique: Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • Feet on Floor: Press your feet firmly into the ground, feel the earth supporting you
  • Cold Water: Splash face or hold ice cubes to reset nervous system

Nature Connection Practices

  • Forest Bathing: 1-2 hours, slow walks in nature
  • Tree Meditation: 5-10 minutes, hands on tree bark
  • Water Grounding: Wade in natural water, ankle-deep
  • Sunrise/Sunset: Watch daily, barefoot if possible
  • Sit on Earth: 15-30 minutes, direct skin contact
